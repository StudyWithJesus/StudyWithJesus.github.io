A – Chains, Sprockets, Belts and Pulleys (4 Questions)

Q1. Chain pitch is defined as the distance between:

  1. The inside and outside plates of the chain
  2. Adjacent roller centres or link pins

Correct answer: b) Adjacent roller centres or link pins

  4. Colour of the original sprocket

Correct answer: d) Colour of the original sprocket

Q3. The letter designation (A, B, C, D, E) for classical V-belts identifies mainly the belt's:

  1. Length
  2. Temperature rating
  3. Width and overall cross-section size
  4. Intended speed range

Correct answer: c) Width and overall cross-section size

Q4. Notched (cogged) V-belts provide an advantage over smooth belts because they:

  1. Are quieter but run hotter
  2. Offer greater flexibility and improved cooling
  3. Can be run with lower tension due to metal cords

Correct answer: b) Offer greater flexibility and improved cooling
